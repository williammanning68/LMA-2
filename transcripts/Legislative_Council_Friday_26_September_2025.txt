Friday 26 September 2025

The President, Mr Farrell, took the Chair at 9.30 a.m., acknowledged the Traditional People and read Prayers. 


POISONS AMENDMENT (INTERSTATE PRESCRIPTIONS) BILL 2025 (No. 45)

First Reading

Bill received from the House of Assembly and read the first time.

Ms RATTRAY (McIntyre - Leader for the Government in the Legislative Council) - Mr President, I move - 

That the second reading of the bill be made an order of the day for Tuesday next.

Motion agreed to.


MESSAGES FROM THE HOUSE OF ASSEMBLY

Joint Sessional Committee on Recommendations of Final Report of the Commission of Inquiry - Re-Establishment - Message Agreed to

Mr PRESIDENT - Honourable members, I have a message from the House of Assembly:

Mr PRESIDENT

The House of Assembly having taken into consideration the message of the Legislative Council dated 25 September 2025 regarding the appointment of a Joint Sessional Committee on the Recommendations of the Final Report of the Commission of Inquiry agrees to the amendments proposed by the Legislative Council to the resolution, and Monday 6 October 2025 at 12 p.m. as time, and Committee Room 2 as the place, for holding the first meeting of the committee.

And further informs the Council it has resolved that:

Mrs Greene;
Mr Jaensch; and
Ms Rosol

be appointed to serve on the Joint Sessional Committee on the part of the House of Assembly.

Jacquie Petrusma
Speaker, House of Assembly
25 September 2025
Joint Select Committee on Energy Matters - Re-Establishment - Message Agreed to

MR PRESIDENT - I have a final message from the House of Assembly:

Mr PRESIDENT

The House of Assembly having taken into consideration the message of the Legislative Council dated 25 September 2025 regarding the appointment of a Joint Select Committee on Energy Matters agrees to the amendments proposed by the Legislative Council to the resolution, and Wednesday 15 October 2025 at 2 p.m. as the time, and Committee Room 1 as the place, for holding the first meeting of the committee.

And further informs the Council it has resolved that:

Mr Bayley;
Mr Garland;
Ms Finlay; and
Mr Vermey

be appointed to serve on the Joint Select Committee on the part of the House of Assembly.

Jacquie Petrusma
Speaker, House of Assembly
25 September 2025


ADJOURNMENT

Ms RATTRAY (McIntyre - Leader for the Government in the Legislative Council) - Mr President, I move - 

That at its rising the Council adjourn until 11 a.m. on Tuesday 4 November 2025.

Motion agreed to.

Ms RATTRAY (McIntyre - Leader for the Government in the Legislative Council) - Mr President, I move - 

That the Council do now adjourn.

The Council adjourned at 9.34 a.m.
